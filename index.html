Political App: Patriota

Patriota is a voter information app designed to provide citizens with an objective view of their candidates running for public office. We support all voters by enabling them to compare one's vision for america with the concrete stances and track records of each candidate.

Activision App: Recess

The Recess app intends to help both parents and children through safe and friendly educational game play. A majority of parents see mobile games as a positive force as it evolves brain learning (87%), invokes creativity (82%), and can improve social skills (65%). Thus, Recess filters and recommends game categories according to parents' intentions.

Non-Profit Website: Rose Haven

As shelter in place continues, domestic violence and homelessness have increased dramatically. My team and I reached out to Rose Haven, a non-profit focused on homeless women and the non-binary, in order to aid those in crisis. We redesigned with visitors in mind, helping folks achieve access to resources and services at greater ease. Simultaneously, we provided a quicker and more heartfelt path for donors desiring to contribute to their community.
